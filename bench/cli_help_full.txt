usage: - [-h] [--enable-continuous-batching] [--max-num-seqs MAX_NUM_SEQS]
         [--max-tokens-per-step MAX_TOKENS_PER_STEP]
         [--prefill-chunk PREFILL_CHUNK]

optional arguments:
  -h, --help            show this help message and exit
  --enable-continuous-batching
                        Enable continuous batching scheduler for streaming
                        requests.
  --max-num-seqs MAX_NUM_SEQS
                        Maximum concurrent sequences handled by the scheduler.
  --max-tokens-per-step MAX_TOKENS_PER_STEP
                        Token budget per scheduler iteration for prefill work.
  --prefill-chunk PREFILL_CHUNK
                        Maximum prompt tokens admitted per sequence during
                        prefill.
